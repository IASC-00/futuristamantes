FM-02 // Manifesto

We don't predict the future.

We remember it — vividly enough to film.

Every place holds a version of itself it hasn't dared become.

We find that version, build it frame by frame, and hand it back as something you can see.

A future you can watch is a future you can choose.

FM-03 // Transmissions

The work, and the work that's coming.

Short by design. Each transmission is a proof — of a place, a brand, or a story rendered as it could be.

FM-04 // Capability

What we make, and how.

Concept films, brand films, documentaries, and social cuts — built on one method that turns an idea into footage.

  1. 01

    Signal

    We listen for the version of a place or brand worth showing — the future already implied by the present.

  2. 02

    Simulation

    We build it: scripted, designed, and rendered into a world with a look you can hold in one frame.

  3. 03

    Alignment

    We tune it with you until the story is true to the room it has to move — funders, residents, customers.

  4. 04

    Execution

    We deliver the cut: film, doc, spot, or vertical — finished, graded, and ready to broadcast.
